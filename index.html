Semantic Enrichment of Villa Lante in ArCo

This project investigates the semantic enrichment of the ArCo Knowledge Graph through the integration of external digital heritage resources. The case study focuses on Villa Lante, one of the most important Italian Renaissance gardens.

Project Abstract

The objective of this project is to identify missing information in the ArCo description of Villa Lante and enrich the knowledge graph with links to external visual and three-dimensional representations.
